How to join MeowcatSoftware


You said you were interested in joining MeowcatSoftware? That's great! Allow me to tell you waht you're getting into.

MeowcatSoftware is, as you probably knew by now, a software development group. We aim to make computer software that makes people's lives easier. It's hard to make good software that everyone likes and that makes everyone's lives easier, which is why we need help from fine people such as yourself. People who want to join MeowcatSoftware should come in with an open mind and be prepared to share your own ideas as well. You may also be asked to participate in interviews, answer surveys, or provide sample work to show you can take the position you're hoping for. We strongly recommend you reach out to us before you apply so that if you have any questions or concerns those can be ironed out before you begin. If you believe you're ready, click the link below to start the process of joining MeowcatSoftware. We're honored to have you with us. Click here to go to the application!
